Removing a player from rotation
Hold down on any player's name to open their options. Mark them as out of rotation for an injured player sitting out, or a development player on limited minutes. They move to a separate section so the rotation stays clean — bring them back any time the same way.
Tracking foul troubleNew
Hold down on a player's name and choose Mark foul trouble — a Foul trouble badge appears on their card as a reminder, on court or bench. This doesn't count individual fouls — it's just a flag for you to keep an eye on. Hold down again to clear it once it's no longer a concern. If a player actually fouls out, mark them out of rotation instead (see above).
Tip: Swap multiple players at once — tap two on court and two on the bench, then hit Substitute to make both changes in one go.
